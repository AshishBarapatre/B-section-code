*CHHATRAPATI SAMBHAJI MAHARAJ*




Full Name: Chhatrapati sambhaji Raje Shivaji Raje Bhosale

Mother: Saibai

Father: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Born: 19/02/1630

Died: 03/04/1680

Place of birth: Fort Purandar


*ABOUT *

  • Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj was the second Chhatrapati of the Maratha Empire. It was the eldest son of Shivaji Maharaj and Maharani Saibai

  • He Ruled for about 9 to 10 years and expanded the empire by fighting various battles. He was known for his bravery, leadership and piety.

  • Aurangzeb brutally tortured them to convert them, but they refused to convert to islam and performed heroic deeds.

*HERITAGE*

Chhatrapati sambhaji Maharaj played a role as a strong pillar of the Maratha Empire. He was a mighty warrior, Skilled ruler and a grat poet.

He expanded the boundaries of the Maratha Empire during his reign, and protected Hinduism.

His Bravery, valiant resistance against Mughal tyranny and great contribution to the building of the Maratha Empire are still remembered with reverence.
